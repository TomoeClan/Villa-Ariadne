One Villa, Two Houses

Two separate houses within one private villa in Porto Heli.

Villa Ariadne is a private villa in Porto Heli, offering two independent, fully equipped houses within the same estate. Guests can enjoy the comfort and privacy of separate living spaces while sharing panoramic sea views and outdoor amenities, including a swimming pool, spacious terraces, a bar area, barbecue facilities, two parking areas, and inviting outdoor lounging areas. They have exclusive access to all amenities during their stay.

The villa is ideal for families, couples traveling together, or small groups who wish to stay close while still enjoying their own space and independence. We also welcome small, trained pets.

Inside the Villa

Poolside House

The Poolside House is the main indoor-outdoor living area of the villa, with direct access to the pool area and the marble patio.

What this house offers

  • Closest position to the swimming pool and sunbathing area
  • Open-plan main area with lounge, dining space, fully equipped kitchen, and sleeping area
  • Separate bedroom with three single beds
  • Easy access to outdoor dining, bar, and barbecue spaces

It works especially well for daytime living, long lunches, and groups who want to stay closely connected to the social heart of the property.

Top Hill House

The Top Hill House is designed in a traditional style, creating a quiet retreat within the same property.

What this house offers

  • Two bedrooms (one with one double bed and one with two single beds)
  • A combined living and dining area with handmade wooden furniture
  • A fully equipped kitchen and bathroom
  • Terrace paved with Karystos stone slabs

Together, the two houses let guests stay close to one another while still enjoying their own rhythm during the day.

Location

Easy access to beaches and Spetses

Porto Heli is a relaxed coastal destination in the Peloponnese, known for its clear waters, marina, and easy access to the island of Spetses. The area combines a laid-back atmosphere with a good range of amenities, including beaches, restaurants, and a lively waterfront, all within a short drive of the villa. Despite its sense of privacy, Porto Heli is only about 2.5 hours' drive from Athens, making it an accessible yet unhurried place to stay by the sea. The villa reflects this balance, offering a calm and private setting while remaining close to the town, beaches, and ferry connections to Spetses.

Porto Heli town About 5 minutes by car
Nearest beach About 2 minutes away
Spetses connections Around 40 minutes (including a 20-minute boat ride)
